The building regulations Approved Document B2, ‘Fire safety: Buildings other than dwellinghouses’, 
defines the occupant capacity of a room, storey, 
building or part of a building as:

The maximum number of persons it is designed to hold; or
The number calculated by dividing the area of room or storey(s) (m²) by a floor space factor 
(m² per person) such as those given in Table C1 of the approved document for guidance.
